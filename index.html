Brands We Carry

Ky Anh Agarwood

- Ky Anh is a supplier of high-quality agarwood products to local and international markets such as Japan, Singapore, China, Taiwan, and United State. Ky Anh agarwood incense are tested in Vietnam to ensure the incense are safe for daily usage, no artificial fragrance or chemical added, and only natural materials are used.

- With 15 years of experience in the agarwood business, Ky Anh is committed in providing best quality natural, safe, and reasonable priced agarwood products to you.

Phuc Linh and Mangala

- Phuc Linh is in the agarwood business for nearly 40 years and continuing the tradition of making agarwood products in Quang Nam, Vietnam. Mangala is a premium brand by Phuc Linh catering to the international markets. Although manual tools have also been replaced by modern machinery to improve the quality and output, Phuc Linh still maintains the manual methods, family secrets to make the products different and unique.

- Phuc Linh and Mangala agarwood incense are tested in Vietnam to ensure the incense are safe for daily usage, no artificial fragrance or chemical added, and only natural materials are used.

Hong Phuc

- Hong Phuc is in the agarwood business since 1038 specializing in making of agarwood incense. Hong Phuc agarwood incense are made from natural materials without adding any artifical perfume, oil or chemical. Hong Phuc is still making incense the traditional way and popular with the locals in Hue, Vietnam. Hong Phuc is a traditional family business, and they currently only serve the domestic market in Vietnam without international presence.

- Hong Phuc agarwood incense are tested in Vietnam to ensure the incense are safe for daily usage, no artificial fragrance or chemical added, and only natural materials are used.
